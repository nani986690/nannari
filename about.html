About Nannari

Nannari, scientifically known as Hemidesmus indicus, is a woody climbing plant native to India and Sri Lanka. Revered in traditional Ayurvedic medicine for centuries, it is widely used in cooling summer beverages.

The roots are sweet, aromatic, and traditionally used to make sharbat that refreshes the body and supports overall wellness during hot climates.

Historical Significance
